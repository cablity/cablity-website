Refund Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. Overview

At Cablity, we strive to provide excellent service to all our customers. This Refund Policy explains our policies regarding refunds, cancellations, and service credits for our internet and cable services.

2. Service Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee for new customers:

  • If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, you may cancel without penalty
  • We will refund any unused portion of your first month's service charge
  • Installation fees are non-refundable unless service could not be installed due to our fault
  • Equipment rental fees will be refunded for unused days

3. Monthly Service Charges

Monthly service charges are billed in advance and are generally non-refundable:

  • No refunds for partial months of service
  • Service continues until the end of your billing cycle after cancellation
  • Exception: Service credits may be issued for extended outages per our Service Level Agreement
  • Pro-rated refunds may be available in specific circumstances at our discretion

4. Installation and Setup Fees

Installation and setup fees are generally non-refundable:

  • Standard installation fees are not refundable once service is installed
  • If installation cannot be completed due to our fault, fees will be refunded
  • Custom installation fees are non-refundable
  • Expedited installation fees are non-refundable

5. Equipment Charges

Equipment-related charges and refunds:

  • Equipment rental fees are refundable for unused days upon return
  • Equipment must be returned in good working condition
  • Damaged or missing equipment will be charged to your account
  • Equipment purchase fees are non-refundable
  • Shipping charges for equipment are non-refundable

6. Service Credits

We may issue service credits for:

  • Extended service outages lasting more than 4 hours
  • Planned maintenance exceeding scheduled windows
  • Service performance issues confirmed by our technical team
  • Billing errors or overcharges

Service credits are applied to your next billing statement and cannot be refunded as cash.

7. Cancellation Policy

To cancel your service:

  • Contact customer service at least 30 days before your desired cancellation date
  • Cancellation requests must be made by the account holder
  • Service continues until the end of your billing cycle
  • All equipment must be returned within 30 days of cancellation
  • Outstanding balances remain due after cancellation

8. Early Termination

For services with contract terms:

  • Early termination fees may apply if you cancel before your contract expires
  • ETF amounts are specified in your service agreement
  • Moving to a service-unavailable area may waive ETF with proper documentation
  • Military deployment orders may waive ETF per applicable laws

9. Refund Processing

When refunds are approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • If original payment method is unavailable, check may be issued
  • Processing times may vary by payment method

10. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  • Request to speak with a supervisor or manager
  • Submit a written complaint to our customer service department
  • Allow 30 days for investigation and response
  • Contact your state's public utilities commission if unresolved

11. Exceptions

This policy does not apply to:

  • Services discontinued due to non-payment
  • Services terminated due to violation of terms
  • Third-party services billed through your Cablity account
  • Services subject to separate refund policies
